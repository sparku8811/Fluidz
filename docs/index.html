Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 10, 2026

Fluidz is built on a foundation of trust and service. We operate under a Zero-Cloud, Zero-Sharing policy. All sensitive data is handled locally on your device.

1. Our Privacy Pledge

Your health and schedule information are sacred. Your information is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party entities. We process everything right where it belongs: on your device.

2. Information Collection & Use

  • Email & SMS: We monitor official VA notifications to extract appointment details via local AI.
  • Privacy Cleansing: Non-scheduling messages (like 2FA codes or spam) are permanently dropped.
  • Calendar: Appointments are placed in a "Shadow Calendar" and only moved to your primary calendar with your explicit approval.

3. Military-Grade Security

All sensitive data is protected using hardware-backed AES-256 encryption. Access is guarded by your device's native biometric authentication (Fingerprint, Face ID, or PIN).

4. Zero Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers. Fluidz does not use tracking pixels or behavior-profiling algorithms.

5. Multi-Device Sync

Device syncing is handled via private, randomized Sync Keys. This process is anonymous and does not require an account or email address.
